FAQ
01What industries do you work with?+
We specialize in e-commerce (Shopify, Amazon, WooCommerce), SaaS companies, startups, and digital agencies. These businesses have unique financial needs — revenue recognition, inventory, subscription billing — that we handle daily.
02I'm behind on my books — can you help?+
Absolutely. Catch-up bookkeeping is one of our most popular services. Whether you're 3 months or 3 years behind, we'll get your books clean and tax-ready. We'll give you a free assessment and timeline upfront.
03Do you file taxes?+
Yes. We prepare and file federal and state tax returns for businesses. Because your books are always clean with us, tax season becomes a smooth, stress-free process — not a mad scramble.
04How quickly will I get my books?+
For ongoing bookkeeping, your books are updated and delivered by the 5th of every month. For catch-up work, we'll give you a specific timeline based on how far back we need to go.
05What software do you use?+
We work with QuickBooks Online and Xero primarily, and can integrate with Shopify, Stripe, Amazon, and most e-commerce platforms. If you're not set up yet, we'll help you get started.
06Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. We keep clients through results, not lock-ins. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
